Amuse is our answer to a simple question; What would a record company look like if it was created today?
Our Service
We have developed a mobile service that we think is easy and engaging to use. We have also established a label with super transparent and fair terms for the artist that enables us to work together to build long and successful artist careers.

We help artists make a living
doing what they love.

The Amuse model is simple
We provide free music distribution to independent artists around the world. After we have made sure the music is available to listeners and fans around the world we use data to identify potential hit tracks and talented artists that we can help grow.
Artist friendly
Our service is easy to use and it’s mobile.
Artists can cancel distribution any time.
We give distributed artists 100% of their royalties.
We don’t charge for distribution.
Signed to our label
When we sign artists we do license deals which means that artists still own their work and after our contract period is over artists are free to do what they want with their music.
Truly digital
The world of music consumption is digital so we created a mobile music distribution app that give our artists total access to their stats and revenues from the palms of their hands.
