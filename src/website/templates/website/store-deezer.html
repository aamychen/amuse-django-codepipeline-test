About Deezer

Deezer is a web-based music streaming service, and is also available on mobile devices. Deezer was founded in Paris, France, the service currently has 40 million licensed tracks in its library, over 30,000 radio channels, 16 million monthly active users, and 5 million paid subscribers as of 6 November 2013.
