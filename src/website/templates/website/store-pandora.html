About Pandora

Pandora gives users personalized radio that plays what they love and continually evolves with their tastes as they use the service. Users may start with the name of an artist, song, genre, or comedian, and create a station. Personalizing listening experience is super simple. If users like a song they can give it a thumbs up, and they will hear more like it. If they don't like it they can give a thumbs down and that song and similar will not play again. Pandora is only available in the U.S., Australia, and New Zealand right now.
